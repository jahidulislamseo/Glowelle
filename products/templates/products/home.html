Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How fast is the delivery?

We offer express delivery within Dhaka city. You can expect your order at your doorstep within 2–4 hours of placing the order. For other areas and outside Dhaka, it takes 24–72 hours.

Are your products 100% genuine & authentic?

Yes! We source all our skincare, cosmetics, and beauty products directly from authorised brand distributors. Every product is 100% genuine, dermatologist-tested, and safe for all skin types.

What is your return policy?

If you are not satisfied with the quality of any product, you can return it immediately at the time of delivery. We have a 'no questions asked' return policy for fresh items.
