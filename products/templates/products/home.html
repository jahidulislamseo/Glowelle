Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How fast is the delivery?

We offer express delivery within Dhaka city. You can expect your grocery at your doorstep within 2-4 hours of placing the order. For other areas, it takes 24-48 hours.

Are your products 100% organic?

Yes! We source our vegetables, fruits, and other products directly from certified organic farms. We ensure zero chemical usage in our supply chain.

What is your return policy?

If you are not satisfied with the quality of any product, you can return it immediately at the time of delivery. We have a 'no questions asked' return policy for fresh items.
