Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can create an account or checkout as a guest.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), bKash, Nogod, and major Credit/Debit cards.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we offer home delivery across Dhaka city and selected areas in Bangladesh.

Can I return my order?

Yes, if you receive a damaged or incorrect product, you can return it at the time of delivery. For other issues, please contact support within 24 hours.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via our "Contact Us" page, email us at support@nyveralife.com, or call our hotline at +8801600818139.
